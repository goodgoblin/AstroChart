Equal Houses with Floating MC/IC

This example demonstrates the new angles feature in AstroChart.

House System: Equal Houses (30° each from Ascendant)

  • Ascendant: 208.12° (28° Libra)
  • MC: 123.50° (3° Leo) - Floats 5° past 10th house cusp
  • 10th House Cusp: 118.12° (28° Cancer)
  • IC: 303.50° (3° Aquarius) - Opposite MC

Notice how the MC and IC axis lines appear at their true astronomical positions, while the house cusps show the Equal house divisions.
